ABOUT US

Welcome to Cutlaries, where we believe that great food is meant to be shared and savored. Located in the heart of California, our family-owned restaurant has been serving up fresh, flavorful dishes since 1980 . We pride ourselves on using only the finest local ingredients, crafting each meal with care, and providing an unforgettable dining experience for every guest. Our menu is a celebration of American classic cuisines , blending traditional recipes with modern twists to bring you something both familiar and exciting. Whether you're here for a quick bite, a cozy dinner with friends, or a special celebration, we strive to create an atmosphere that feels like home. At Cutlaries, we’re not just about food—we’re about creating memories. Our dedicated team is passionate about giving you the best service and a warm, welcoming environment. We can't wait to have you join us for your next meal!
